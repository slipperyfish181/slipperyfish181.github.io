EPS Soccer

EPS soccer happens during spring trimester. I think it is important to play sports during highschool as it builds relationship and community. Also, I enjoy playing soccer and it makes me forget about school and think about soccer, something I love.

Communtiy

EPS soccer has a great community and brings a lot of different people together with a common interest. Community is super important to me and soccer is a way where I can connect with many different people.

Location

Soccer practice takes place at Wiburton park everyday from 3:30 to 5:00. We are taken to practice in an EPS bus and can either be taken back to EPS or are parents can pick us up.

Enjoyment

Soccer is super fun as it is something I really enjoy. I have been playing soccer for almost my whole life and always have fun while playing.
