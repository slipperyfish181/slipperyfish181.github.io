Chace's Pancake Corral is celebrating 65 years of doing what we love, making pancakes! If you are looking for a Traditional dining experience we are the place to visit. Our recipes have never changed to ensure the feeling of the old days at our restaurant.

Our main goal is to celebrate Chace in the pancake corral through our cooking and hospitality. We use the same recipes from the opening of our restaruant in 1958 and will continue to do so until the corral eventually closes. Chace did so much for the community of Bellevue and think of the recipes as a way to remember who Chace was.

We have kept the same interior to the pancake corral since it was first opened. The tables and chairs have stayed the same, giving the restaurant the extra traditional feel. Coming to Chace's Pancake Corral is a Bellevue tradition and we want to keep it that way. We keep tradition to remember the Chace, the founder of the pancake corral. Chace believed in friendship and fellowship and we know that his legacy is carried on in the food and feeling of family in the pancake corral.
